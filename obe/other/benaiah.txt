====== Benaiah ======

===== Definition: =====

Benaiah, Son of Jehoiadah, was one of King David's faithful officers.

  * When King David proclaimed Solomon king, Benaiah was a key person in the transition.
  * King Solomon appointed Benaiah to be commander of his army.
  * A number of other priests and warriors by the same name are mentioned in Chronicle and Kings.

(See also: other)
