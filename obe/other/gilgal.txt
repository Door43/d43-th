====== Gilgal ======

===== Facts: =====

"Gilgal", meaning circle of stones, was a name applied to several towns in the Old Testament.

  * The Gilgal near Jericho was the political, religous and military center of the tribe. of Benjamin.
  * The Gilgal of Elijah and Elisha was the departure point of the prophets as they crossed the Jordon when Elijah was taken up to heaven.
  * There are other references to Gilgals which are questionable.
