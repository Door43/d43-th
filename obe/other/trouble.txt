====== trouble ======

===== Definition: =====

As utilized in Job, troubles are problems and serious difficulties.

  * In the Bible troubles are not merely aggravating problems but times of testing that God uses to shape a person of faith.
  * The Bible indicates that troubles (trials and tests) are a normal part of the life of a believing person.
