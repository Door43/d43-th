====== grain offering ======

===== Definition: =====

A grain offering was given by the donor primarily as an act of worship and recognitio of God's provision.

  * The offering would usually be either wheat or barley, finely ground, cooked or uncooked.
  * Oil and salt had to be added with no yeast or honey permited.
  * Usually given after a burnt offering.
  *
